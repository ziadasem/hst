About us

What We Do

Exceptional quality care with your privacy in mind; Home Sleep Study of Texas is a freestanding outpatient facility that provides exceptional ambulatory sleep services & ventilatory treatments, combining the latest technology in sleep medicine with knowledgeable, well-trained staff focused on patient comfort and privacy.

We are committed confidentially regarding your health care needs and the treatment of other patients. Our team of devoted healthcare professionals along with our board certified sleep physicians provide all patients with specific personalized methods for their care, primarily focusing on infusible and injectable services.

Treatments

Partnering with a team of board certified sleep physicians revered by their peers in the field of medicine; we use their guidance and training principles to offer the best quality therapy that money can buy.

Devices

We carry the most up to date PAP devices offered on the market. Each device is clinically determined based on your treatment needs and the settings are set by our sleep physicians.

HST

A minimalistic design allowing for a comfortable study in your own home! This home study kit covers all aspects necessary to properly screen and diagnose Obstructive sleep apnea.

PSG

In depth exam performed by our registered polysomnographic technologist at our state of the art facilities . Covers all aspects of the body during nocturnal hours. With this exam we cover EEG, EKG, EMG, breathing and pulse oximetry. Polysomnogram is used world wide in order to help diagnose over 300 sleep disorders!

PAP Titrations

Named the “gold standard” in treatment for sleep disordered breathing by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine. This effective treatment is done in house at our sleep centers allowing the registered sleep technologist to place the patient on positive airway pressure to help find the optimal therapeutic recommendation setting to keep your airway open while you sleep.
